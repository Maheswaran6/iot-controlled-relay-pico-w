Home

This project focuses on developing a low-cost, portable water testing device that helps people quickly check basic water quality parameters such as pH and turbidity. The device is designed especially for rural and semi-urban areas.

Problem Statement

In many rural areas, access to safe drinking water is a major challenge. Laboratory-based water testing is expensive and time-consuming. As a result, people often consume water without knowing whether it is safe or contaminated.

Proposed Solution

We propose a portable water testing device that uses low-cost sensors to measure basic water quality parameters. The device provides instant results using LEDs or a simple display, making it easy for anyone to use.

How It Works

  1. Water sample is placed in the testing container.
  2. Sensors measure parameters such as pH and turbidity.
  3. A microcontroller processes the sensor data.
  4. Results are displayed using LEDs or a mobile interface.
